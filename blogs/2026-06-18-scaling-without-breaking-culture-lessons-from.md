Insights into maintaining company culture during rapid growth, drawing from WunderGraph's in-person Madrid Work Week gathering of 31 people across 12 countries. The piece covers intentional culture-building practices including rotating lunch groups, gratitude recognition, and deliberate unstructured time for meaningful connection.
